# Color Description
1 Green is grass, which multiplies once in 5 ticks, dies when grass eater eats him.
2 Yellow is grass eater, which multiplies after eating 4 grass and dies after his energy drops below 0.
3 Red is predator, which multiplies after eating 5 grass, or 4 grass eaters, dies after his energy drops below 0.
4 This one is human, who multiplies after eating 5 grass, or 4 grass eaters, can't eat predators. Dies after his energy drops below 0. Eats grass or grass eaters depending on amount of energy (energy less than 30 - grass, more - grass eater).
5 Purple is Hermit Mushroom, which destroys any living creature (grass eater/predator, except human) he sees in nearby 8 fields. Can't multiply or die. Finally works after a lot of sufferings :)
